le Blog de Léonore

le Blog de Léonore is a French name, although my proficiency in the language is limited. I chose it to add more features to the website. The majority of the content on the website will be written in Chinese, with some written in English as well.

Since Léonore /leonɔ:r/ is a French name, it should be pronounced with the French accent /r/, the uvular consonant. Of course, you can absolutely use the American pronunciation /'li:ənɔ:/.

The top-level domain .top was chosen simply because it is inexpensive. Perhaps it will be changed to .com, .net or .org in the future.

I didn’t intentionally search for a particular French name. Instead, I combined random letters and added French syllables to create a unique name. Interestingly, even in English-speaking countries, Leonore is relatively rare and few people use it. Furthermore, it is a feminine name and while it may be less popular among girls, it is quite distinctive. Now that the name has been chosen, I believe it’s best to stick with it.

I created this website solely based on my personal interests, with the intention of sharing techniques and knowledge that I have learned from the internet or through my research. Although there are some areas of the website that are still in the early stages of development, I am committed to improving and refining the content over time.

The goal of this website is to share something awesome that will add a bit of enjoyment to your life. By subscribing to le Blog de Léonore, you can receive alerts when new content is posted. Additionally, you can leave comments in the designated section, where providing a nickname and an Email address is requiredto protect your privacy and verify that the commenter is a real person. Your Email address will not be displayed publicly. Comments should be respectful and relevant to the topic at hand, and should not include spam or promotional content. Let’s work together to make le Blog de Léonore even better.


2023-03-31更新

斥巨资（≈$10/y）换了个域名，原来的leonore.top到期后就不续了。经过精心寻找设计的昵称最后发现仅仅是个自娱自乐的玩笑，最后还是得回归到自己的本名。

其实迟早知道要换，只不过没有预料到这么早就换了。原来期望的是换一个.com、.net或者.org的（“Perhaps it will be changed to .com, .net or .org in the future.”），最好是xxm或者xiao的，但找了几圈不是太贵就是已经被注册了。其他的考虑过xxm.red，这是最开始的选择，也是我比较喜欢的（“物美价廉”），但考虑到.red一般人不会以为是顶级域名就放弃了。退而求其次，考虑把名字加长一点。资金方面只能承受<$20/y，有以下一些类型：xmxiao、xiaoxm、xmshaw等等，这些的大多在$10-20。找了一段时间突然发现少一个字母也是差不多的价格，就把xxiao作为最后的选择。可能由于本名的特殊性，拼音的域名确实不好选，但双x也可以看作一个feature。

三个流行的通用顶级域名只剩下.org，说到底这个选择还是各种妥协。当然.com虽然最流行，但总感觉带有commercial的含义太重。本身 le Blog de Léonore 最初的定义更多的是一个记录分享的公共网络平台，所以.net应该是最合适的，也是我最想要的，但就是已被抢注。参考过一些其他Bolg或者个人网站的域名，除了.com最常见外，.org和.net的数量不相上下。.net更流行也许可能是.net也是一些互联网企业服务提供商的选择（如Speedtest）。我也找到一些.org的网站，比如经常用的Telegram，Wikipedia，还有联合国UN，Archive，Mozilla等等都是选择.org作为显式的主域名。虽然都是些国际性组织，但至少能给我些许慰藉。于是最后选择了.org组合xxiao，尽管不是最好的选择，也不确定以后会不会再心血来潮换过一个，但我希望能保持地久一点，最好找一个能说服自己的寓意。：）
